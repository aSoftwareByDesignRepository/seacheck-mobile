SeaCheck is a deck-side companion to paper charts for small-boat coastal sailing — not a replacement for official nautical charts.

Use it for boat speed, coordinates, a quick chart position without going below, passage sketches, and anchor alarms. Aid to navigation only; not a certified chart plotter. Maps use OpenSeaMap / OpenStreetMap volunteer data and may be outdated. Always carry official, corrected nautical charts.

Features:
• GPS on chart — COG, SOG, bearing
• Passage planning, waypoints, GPX export
• Offline region packs (Wi‑Fi download)
• Anchor, arrival, and off-course alarms
• Track recording
• English and German UI

Free. No ads. No analytics. Data stays on your device.
