## 2. What SeaCheck is

SeaCheck is a **navigation aid** for recreational and coastal sailing. It helps with:

- Showing your GPS position on a chart
- Passage planning, waypoints, and tracks
- Anchor, arrival, and off-course alarms
- Offline chart packs (base map + community seamarks)

SeaCheck is **not** a certified electronic chart display (ECDIS), chart plotter, or replacement for **official nautical charts**.

---

## 3. Map data & third-party services

SeaCheck builds on the volunteer work of **OpenSeaMap** and **OpenStreetMap**, plus **CARTO** Voyager base tiles. That community data is valuable for orientation and route planning.

The data is maintained by volunteers and **may be outdated or incomplete**. It is not a surveyed nautical chart and does not include official depths, fairways, or corrections from hydrographic offices.

**Because of this, SeaCheck is not suitable for navigation on its own.** Use it alongside your official, corrected, up-to-date charts. Check Notices to Mariners and verify anything safety-critical before you go.
